Users:

Here are the users in this project:
  1. DBA
  2. Middle-level user
  3. Casual and naive user

DBA:

The database admin is one of the key users of the database. The role of DBA is becoming an increasingly important one with the information driven environment of today. Database admin is responsible for understanding and managing the overall database environment by developing, implementing and maintaining a blueprint to follow when deploying databases in the organization. A database admin determines the structure of the database and how data is stored within the database. They must also constantly monitor various resources. Without the DBA's oversight, slowdowns, downtime and system outages will inevitably occur. This is not ideal especially in a business environment.


Middle-leve user:

The employees are the middle level users. Their main function revolves around listening to the passenger's needs and requesting the DBA for changes in the Passengers information if required. They can also use the database for extracting passengers' information for enhancement of the passenger's experience. After getting permission from the DBA, the employees in the managerial positions can use the database for managing the employees details. Their job includes constantly querying and updating the database, using standard types of queries and updates that have been carefully programmed and tested. Another important function of the middle-level users is the management of each flight, that is required for the smooth functioning of the airlines.


Casual and naive user:

Casual user: These are the users who occasionally access the database but they require different information each time. They use a sophisticated database query language basically to specify their request and are typically middle or level managers or other occasional browsers Naive user: Clerks in any bank is a naive user because they don't have any DBMS knowledge but they still use the database and perform their given task.
